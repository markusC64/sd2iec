Fuses and lock bits settings
(Arduino's defaukt settings)
 
Arduino Mega 2560 (with ATmega2560)

Low fuses      = 0xFF
High fuses     = 0xD8
Extended fuses = 0xFD
Lock Bits      = 0x3F

Arduino Mega  or Arduino Mega 1280 (with ATmega1280)

Low fuses      = 0xFF
High fuses     = 0xDA
Extended fuses = 0xF5
Lock Bits      = 0x3F
